About Us

The Cookery School was created as a platform for people to see existing delicious recipes as well as users being able to sumbit their own. The recipes are simple, all with 15 ingredients or less and take about 30 minutes to make on average. We are constantly updating the website with new recipes and ideas to bring to your kitchen at home. Please follow us on Facebook, Instagram and Twitter to receive more updates and hear what we have been doing.


We aim to ensure all our recipes will cost you £10 or less to show you that there is no need to break the bank to make these yummy meals for all family and friends.
