Mains

The Best Spaghetti and Meatballs

Ingredients

For the meatballs
  • 400g beef mince
  • 400g pork mince
  • 100g ciabatta, sourdough or other white bread, crusts removed, torn into pieces
  • 100ml milk
  • 6 garlic cloves, very finely chopped or grated
  • large handful parsley leaves, finely chopped
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 tbsp dried oregano
  • 50g grated parmesan and extra to serve
For the sauce
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely sliced
  • pinch of golden caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • splash of red wine (optional)
  • 3 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 500g spaghetti, to serve

Method

  1. In a bowl, put in beef and pork and season generously
  2. Place bread in bowl with milk to soak and then add to meat
  3. Add all remaining ingredients to bowl
  4. Roll meatballs into a ball and chill for 60 minutes
  5. Fry the meatballs in batches with 3tbsp oil in large frying pan then set aside
  6. Add all sauce into ingredients into frying pan for 10 mins
  7. Place meatballs back in frying pan and summer on low heat for 20 mins
  8. Remove from heat then cook spaghetti. Divide into bowls and serve!
